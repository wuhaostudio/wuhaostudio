## 🧠 About Me

A perpetual explorer captivated by the **beauty of mathematics** and the **wonder of AI**. I believe elegant formulas are the cornerstone of understanding intelligence, and I'm passionate about constructing and deciphering "thinking" machines from first principles.

🧠 **An Apostle of Thought**: Enjoy transforming vague intuition into rigorous mathematical formulation.  
⚛️ **A Seeker of First Principles**: Fascinated by the simple, profound principles underlying complex models.  
📈 **An Artisan of Optimization**: Navigating the landscapes of loss functions, seeking paths that are more optimal, faster, and robust.  
🌌 **An Explorer of the Unknown**: Possess a childlike curiosity about agents, reasoning, and the emergent behaviors of models.  
🔍 **A Contributor to the Commons**: In the galaxy of open source, I am both a learner and aspire to be a contributor of a tiny spark.

> Welcome to my digital study. Here documents every trivial yet thrilling discovery in the cycle of "Math → Algorithm → Implementation".
